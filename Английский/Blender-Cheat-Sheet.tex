		\huge \textbf{Friction}
	\end{center}
	
	\section*{Formulae}
	
	$$F_F = \mu F_N$$
	
	$\mu$~--- coefficient of friction; $\mu \in [0; 1]$.
	
	\section*{Types of friction}
	
	\textbf{Static}: $\mu_s$.
	
	\noindent \textbf{Kinetic}: $\mu_k$.
	
	$$\mu_s \geqslant \mu_k$$
	
	\section*{Unity}
	
	Project settings $\rightarrow$ Physics $\rightarrow$ Enable Enhanced Determination $\rightarrow$ Two Directional Type.
	
	In Unity dynamic friction equals kinetic friction.
	
	RMB $\rightarrow$ Physic Material $\rightarrow$ Dynamic and Static friction settings. Also has different combines type (average, minimum, multiply, maximum). The priority order: Average $<$ Minimum $<$ Multiply $<$ Maximum.
	
	On an inclined plane $\mu$ should be no more then $\tan$ if there are no external forces.
